Admission Procedure

Learn about the steps to join our school community.

Admission Overview

Our school offers a comprehensive and straightforward admission process to welcome new students. We are committed to providing a smooth and transparent experience for both students and their families.

We encourage prospective students to apply early and familiarize themselves with our admission requirements and deadlines.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must have completed the previous grade with a minimum grade requirement.
  • All students must submit their academic transcripts and proof of residency.
  • For certain grades, entrance tests may be required.
  • Applicants should provide letters of recommendation if applicable.

Application Process

  1. Submit Application Form: Complete the online application form available on our website.
  2. Submit Documents: Upload all required documents including academic transcripts and proof of residency.
  3. Entrance Test (if applicable): Schedule and attend the entrance test, if required.
  4. Interview: Participate in an interview with our admissions team.
  5. Receive Admission Decision: Wait for the admission decision to be communicated via email.
  6. Enrollment: Complete the enrollment process and attend orientation.

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: June 30, 2024
  • Entrance Test Date: July 15, 2024
  • Interview Dates: July 20-25, 2024
  • Admission Decision Announced: August 1, 2024
  • Enrollment Deadline: August 15, 2024
