About Our School

Shaping the Future with Quality Education

Our History

The school was established in 1924, it was managed by six sisters - Sr. Innocenza Vallino, Sr. Cecilia Da Roit, Sr. Clotilde Appiano, Sr. Maria Bricarello, Sr. Rosetti Antonietta and Sr. Giulia Berra.

Our commitment to education and personal growth has always been at the core of our mission. We pride ourselves on nurturing well-rounded individuals who excel both academically and personally.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a supportive and challenging environment where students can achieve their full potential and become lifelong learners. We strive to foster academic excellence, creativity, and integrity.

Our Vision

We envision a school where every student is prepared for a successful future through innovative teaching methods and a commitment to holistic education. Our vision is to inspire students to be leaders and positive contributors to society.

Our Values and Goals

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and ethical behavior.
  • Excellence: We strive for academic and personal excellence in all our endeavors.
  • Respect: We foster a culture of respect and inclusivity within our school community.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovative teaching practices to enhance learning outcomes.
  • Community: We are committed to building strong partnerships with our community and stakeholders.
